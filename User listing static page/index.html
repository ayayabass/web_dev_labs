About Mumbo-jumbo

Mumbo-jumbo is a horror jRPG game inspired by Hello Charlotte. It is a story-driven game where you play as a young girl named Charlotte who discovers the dark secrets of her family's past. With a unique art style and haunting soundtrack, Mumbo-jumbo is a must-play for any horror game fan.

Gameplay

Mumbo-jumbo features turn-based combat and exploration through a variety of environments. As you progress through the game, you will encounter puzzles and battles that will test your skills and wits. With multiple endings and a branching narrative, your choices will determine Charlotte's fate.
